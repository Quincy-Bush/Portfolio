Quincy.m.bush@gmail.com 507-884-9868 LinkedIn
Overview
  • Through a networking opportunity developed with past coworker at Surly Brewing, I had the opportunity to work as an independent engineer via contract to streamline and produce code for the effiecent use of proprietary software DJ Event Planner, with local music company Transmission Music.
  • My focus was on streamlining the administrative end-user and client-based services to help Transmission music employees begin efficiently using DJ Event Planner to meet company goals and deadlines.
Key Responsibilities and Details
  • Worked independently and collaboratively to develop and implement solutions for automated email responses and content for various client scenerios and schedules, Configuring outgoing and incoming email servers as well as distributions for necessary employees and clients.
  • Utilized HTML, plain script, and XML to create or redefine the structure, content, accessibility, and frequency of client-end services such as emails, events, calendars, schedules, and portal interfaces.
  • Met directly with Transmission Music DJs and administrators to ensure quality standards and goals are met for emails and SMS sent to employees and clients from proprietary software DJ Event Planner
Current Focus
